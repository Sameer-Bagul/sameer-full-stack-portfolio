# Design System for Sameer Portfolio

## Colors
- **Background / Base**: `#151312` (Dark charcoal, used for all page backgrounds)
- **Primary Accent**: `#C5FF41` (Neon lime/yellow, used for primary CTAs and active highlights)
- **Secondary Accent**: `#F46C38` (Vibrant orange, used for hover states and secondary elements)
- **Neutral/Borders/Cards**: `#B6B4BD`, `#999999`, `#888888`, `#767676`, `#6A6B6E` (Variations of grays for card backgrounds, borders, and muted text)
- **Error/Alert**: `#FF2600`, `#0000EE`

## Typography
- **Headings & Display**: `Seona` (Used sparingly for Hero Titles, Section Headers, and Logo)
- **Body & General UI**: `Poppins` (Used for paragraphs, standard buttons, and navigation)

## Styling & Layout
- Dark mode by default, background is always `#151312`.
- Glassmorphic card styling with subtle `#6A6B6E` borders.
- Unified, consistent spacing, high-end modern layout.

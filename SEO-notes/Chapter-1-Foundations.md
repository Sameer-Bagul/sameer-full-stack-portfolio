# Chapter 1: The Foundations of Modern SEO

SEO is no longer about "tricking" search engines; it's about providing the most relevant, high-quality answer to a user's query.

## 1. The Three Pillars

### I. Technical SEO
The infrastructure of your site. If Google can't crawl or index you, your content doesn't matter.
*   **Sitemaps & Robots.txt**: Providing a map and rules for crawlers.
*   **HTTPS**: Security is a primary ranking signal.
*   **Site Structure**: Flat vs Hierarchical architectures.

### II. On-Page SEO
The quality and structure of your content.
*   **Metadata**: Titles and Descriptions that drive CTR (Click-Through Rate).
*   **Header Hierarchy**: Proper use of `<h1>` through `<h6>`.
*   **Keyword Placement**: Natural integration in the first 100 words.

### III. Off-Page SEO
The authority and reputation of your site.
*   **Backlinks**: Links from other authoritative sites (E-E-A-T).
*   **Social Signals**: Brand mentions across the web.

## 2. Universal Search Intent
Users search for four things:
1.  **Informational**: "How to learn Next.js"
2.  **Navigational**: "Sameer Bagul Portfolio"
3.  **Commercial Investigation**: "Best AI developer in Pune"
4.  **Transactional**: "Hire freelance AI developer"

**Deep Strategy**: Your site should have separate landing zones for ALL four intents.

# Chapter 4: Content Discovery & Semantic SEO

Semantic SEO is about building topical authority, not just ranking for a single keyword.

## 1. Topical Authority
If you want to be known for "AI Development", you must write about Python, PyTorch, LLMs, and RAG. This builds a "Cluster" of related content.

## 2. Linking for Crawlability
If a page isn't linked, it doesn't exist to a crawler.
*   **Related Research Modules**: Links at the bottom of notes to other related notes.
*   **Deep Linking**: Linking from a blog post directly to a specific project.

## 3. Keyword Clustering
Don't target "Freelancer" on every page.
*   **Home Page**: Broad persona ("Software AI Developer").
*   **Notes Page**: Educational keywords ("Coding Notes", "Technical Guide").
*   **Projects Page**: Capability keywords ("MERN Stack Examples", "AI SaaS Projects").
